Introduction

Ruby Taiwan is the heart of the Ruby programming community in Taiwan. Established in 2008, we are passionate about fostering connections and sharing the beauty of Ruby with enthusiasts globally. Come join us at our monthly gathering, Ruby Jam, and our (mostly) annual event, Rubyconf Taiwan!
